# REIT Analysis Blueprint

## 1. Core Question

How can finance theory be used to understand REIT valuation, portfolio risk, and sector specialization?

## 2. Educational Positioning

LY-STScope Ver.2 should be presented as a finance education and analytics project. It should avoid language that sounds like direct investment advice.

Recommended positioning:

> LY-STScope Ver.2 helps users study REIT valuation and portfolio risk through real market examples.

## 3. REIT-Specific Metrics

- Dividend Yield: annual dividend divided by current price.
- FFO: Funds From Operations, a common REIT operating cash flow measure.
- AFFO: Adjusted Funds From Operations, often used to evaluate sustainable dividends.
- Price / FFO: a REIT valuation multiple similar in spirit to P/E, but more suitable for REITs.
- AFFO Payout Ratio: dividends divided by AFFO.
- NAV Premium / Discount: market price compared with estimated net asset value.
- Debt / EBITDA: leverage and financing risk indicator.
- Occupancy Rate: property utilization indicator.

## 4. Valuation Framework

### Income Approach

Dividend discount or AFFO-based cash flow approach.

### Asset Approach

NAV-based valuation using property asset value minus debt.

### Market Approach

Peer group multiples such as Price / FFO, dividend yield spread, and sector peer comparison.

## 5. Risk Framework

- Interest-rate sensitivity
- Leverage risk
- Sector concentration
- Tenant and occupancy risk
- Portfolio covariance and correlation

## 6. Customer-Focused Scorecard

The app should translate REIT data into five practical lenses:

- Dividend Safety Score: dividend yield, AFFO payout, debt burden, occupancy, and lease duration.
- FFO/AFFO Valuation Score: Price/FFO, NAV premium or discount, dividend yield, and payout burden.
- Debt & Rate Risk Score: Debt/EBITDA, beta, and estimated sensitivity to interest rates.
- Property Quality Score: occupancy, tenant quality, and rent growth.
- Growth Score: FFO growth, rent growth, and operating quality.

## 7. Future Expansion

- REIT peer group engine by property type
- Simple equal-weight REIT portfolio backtest
- Treasury yield sensitivity dashboard
- REIT versus broad equity market comparison
- Geographic exposure and property-type exposure ontology
