ToxiGuard-Platform is a regulatory strategy and scientific decision-support
tool. It is not a substitute for qualified expert review, validated laboratory
testing, official regulatory guidance review, or agency interaction.

This platform is not affiliated with, endorsed by, certified by, or approved by

## QSAR and ICH M7 Use

QSAR and structural-alert outputs are preliminary. ICH M7 assessments require
expert review, applicability-domain evaluation, complementary methodology
where appropriate, source traceability, and case-specific justification.

## Bioequivalence and Dissolution Use

The f2 and bootstrap calculations are screening and strategy-support outputs.
Final bioequivalence strategy must consider the product-specific FDA guidance,
dosage form, release mechanism, BCS class, strength proportionality,
formulation differences, method conditions, variability, and the full
submission context.

## USP/EP/Compendial Use

USP, EP, and other pharmacopeial references must be verified against the
current official licensed monograph or authorized source. This repository must
not be treated as an official compendial source.

## Data Accuracy

Public databases and APIs can be incomplete, delayed, unavailable, rate-limited,
or inconsistent. Users should confirm critical values against primary sources
before using outputs in regulatory, clinical, manufacturing, or commercial
decisions.
