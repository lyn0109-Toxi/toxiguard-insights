## ⚖️ Regulatory & R&D Implications
- **Beyond Copilots**: While 2024-2025 were the years of AI "copilots" (passive assistants), 2026 marks the rise of **AI Agents**. These agents don't just suggest molecules; they autonomously manage API interactions, laboratory experiment planning, and regulatory data reconciliation.
- **Infrastructure Shift**: As noted by WSJ and confirmed by AMD CEO Lisa Su, "Agentic AI" requires significantly more CPU power for logical orchestration compared to traditional generative models. This has direct implications for biopharma companies investing in on-premise or hybrid-cloud high-performance computing (HPC) for secure drug discovery.
