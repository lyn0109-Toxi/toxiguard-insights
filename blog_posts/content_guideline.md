# Daily Pharma Blog Content Generation Guidelines

## 🎯 Core Strategy
- **Hook & Keywords**: Use trending keywords from global business and health news (e.g., Daily Trend) to attract the target audience (executives, investors, researchers).
- **Substance**: The actual content must be strictly evidence-based, objective, and verified.

## ⚖️ Strict Data Verification Rules
1. **Clinical Data**: Any mention of efficacy (ORR, PFS, OS), patient enrollment numbers, or trial phases MUST be referenced to **ClinicalTrials.gov** (provide the NCT number if possible) or official peer-reviewed publications.
2. **Regulatory Milestones**: Approval statuses, BLA/NDA submissions, or PDUFA dates MUST be sourced from **FDA Open Documents** (e.g., FDA press releases, AdCom briefing documents) or EMA equivalents.
3. **Safety & Quality**: Any mention of safety profiles, toxicology, or manufacturing standards MUST align with and reference specific **ICH Guidelines** (e.g., ICH M3, ICH Q-series, ICH E-series).
4. **Calculations & Market Sizing**: Any financial or market projection must clearly state its calculation basis or cite the primary source (e.g., SEC 10-K filings, official company earnings reports). Avoid AI hallucinations of market sizes.

## 📝 Markdown Structure Template
```markdown
# [Daily Trend Hook]: [Specific Medical/Clinical Topic] (YYYY-MM-DD)

## 📌 Context (Daily Trend)
- Brief summary of why this is trending today based on global health news.

## 📊 Clinical Evidence & Trial Data
| Phase | Asset (NCT Number) | Mechanism / Target | Validated Data (ORR, PFS, etc.) | Primary Source |
|-------|--------------------|--------------------|---------------------------------|----------------|
| ...   | ...                | ...                | ...                             | ClinicalTrials.gov / FDA |

## ⚖️ Regulatory & Safety Implications
- Analysis based strictly on FDA guidelines or ICH safety/toxicology standards.

## 📈 Financial & Strategic Impact
- Verified financial implications (using 10-K data or explicit calculation logic).
```
